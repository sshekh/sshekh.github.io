Programming Club IIT Kanpur:                                        

Website: http://pclub.in/site/


Programming Club (or PClub) is a community of students enthusiastic about coding, ethical hacking, Web Designing and all other aspects of programming. It is a part of the Science and Technology Council under Students Gymkhana. Facebook group of Pclub has grown to a size of 14K members as an active Q and A forum.  The club motto is “Eat | Sleep | Code”. PClub boasts of a very passionate, talented and earnest group of people.

Structure:

PClub team involves a group of 3 - 4 coordinators (3rd year undergraduates, preferably from different halls) 13 - 17 secretaries (second year undergraduates) and 2 - 3 PG mentors. Tenure for PClub team is about one year.

Activities:

Activities organised by the club can be broadly organised into Development (Web Development, Open Source, Game/App Development) and programming contests. Lecture series are regularly taken on algorithms, web hijacking, game development and android app development platforms. Club has different subgroups like the “Algorithms Interests Group” and “Navya” - the open source development community. Apart from activities for its members, club also develops various applications to be used by the campus junta like Puppy Love (during valentine week) and Linux Install fest.

Summer Camp:

During summers, PClub offers a variety of projects to the first year students. The purpose of these projects is to act as a great learning experience for them as well as to expose them to software development. Club has kept an archive of projects previously completed so that students can build upon them.  Lectures on algorithmic programming are taken throughout the semester as well as in the summer. Content of the lecture series developed during summer has been developed into a blog which has received more than 35K views as of date. Weekend Programming Contests are held regularly at its own online judge  to increase the interest of students in competitive programming as well as to improve their practice. PClub members have given excellent performance in various competitions like ICPC world finals, Techfest (IITK won all the programming competitions at Techfest 2013), etc.
